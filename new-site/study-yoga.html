Frequently Asked Questions

Is this suitable for all subjects?
Yes! The techniques are universal and work for Math, Science, Literature, History, Languages—all subjects. We teach methods that optimize how your brain learns any material.
Will it really reduce exam anxiety?
Study Yoga teaches proven stress management and exam strategies, not medical treatment. Students report feeling more confident and prepared. With proper technique and preparation, anxiety naturally decreases.
How quickly will I see improvement?
Many students feel more focused immediately after Day 1. Grade improvement typically shows within 2-3 weeks of consistent practice. Best results come from following the techniques regularly.
Can I attend if I'm already doing well in studies?
Absolutely! Good students often become excellent students by optimizing their already strong study habits with our techniques.
What if I'm behind in my studies?
Study Yoga is perfect for catching up. Our techniques help you master material faster and retain it longer, making it ideal for students playing catch-up.
Is it just brain exercises or actual study techniques?
Both! We combine brain activation exercises with practical study methods. Brain exercises prepare your mind; study techniques teach you HOW to study effectively.
Will parents understand what I'm learning?
Yes! We provide materials and explanations for parents too. Many parents attend Day 1 to understand the program and support their child's learning at home.
When is the next workshop?
Workshops are scheduled regularly, especially before board exams and competitive exam seasons. Contact us to know the current schedule for your location.
