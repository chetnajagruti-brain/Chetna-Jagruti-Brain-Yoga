Frequently Asked Questions

🤔 How do I get started with the programs?

Simply fill out the contact form above or call us on WhatsApp at +91 91753 16756. Our counselor will guide you through the process, help you choose the right program, and answer all your questions. You can also visit us at any of our centers for a personal consultation.

❓ What is the DMIT Test?

DMIT (Dermatoglyphics Multiple Intelligence Test) is a scientific analysis of fingerprints that reveals your natural talents, abilities, and intelligence types. It helps identify your strengths, learning style, and suitable career paths scientifically and non-invasively.

👧 What age groups do you serve?

We serve students from elementary school to college level. Each program is customized based on the student's age and academic level. Even adults benefit from Brain Yoga and life skills training. Contact us to know which program is best for your age group.

💰 What are the program fees?

Program fees vary based on the duration, format, and location. We offer flexible options including group classes, batch programs, and personalized sessions. Contact us directly for detailed pricing information and available packages. Pricing on enquiry only.

🗓️ What is the duration of each program?

Program duration varies: Brain Yoga (4-12 weeks), Chetna Jagruti (12-24 weeks), Study Yoga (4-12 weeks or exam-specific), and DMIT Test (single session plus analysis). Each program can be customized to fit your schedule.

📍 Can I attend programs online?

We offer both in-person and hybrid formats for most programs. Some techniques are best practiced in-person, while theory and guidance can be online. Contact us to discuss the format that works best for you.

🧠 How will I see results from Brain Yoga?

Most students notice improvements in focus and mental clarity within 2-3 weeks. Significant improvements in memory and stress levels typically appear within 4-6 weeks. Results depend on regular practice and consistent effort.

📚 How does Study Yoga improve exam performance?

Study Yoga combines brain optimization techniques with proven study strategies. Students learn effective study methods, time management, exam techniques, and stress management. This results in 30-50% faster learning and better exam performance.
