Chetna Jagruti is an awakening—a mindfulness and wellness workshop designed for busy adults. Through guided breathing techniques, mindfulness practices, physical exercises, and meditation, we help you manage stress, gain mental clarity, enhance focus, and boost productivity.

No prior experience needed. Whether you're juggling career pressures, family responsibilities, or health concerns, Chetna Jagruti meets you where you are.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any prior experience?
Not at all! Chetna Jagruti is designed for complete beginners. No yoga, meditation, or wellness experience necessary. Our instructors guide you step-by-step.
Is it suitable for people with health issues?
Chetna Jagruti is a wellness program, not medical treatment. However, most practices can be adapted for different physical conditions. Please inform us of any health concerns during registration so we can provide modifications.
What if I can't commit to all sessions?
We offer flexible attendance. Many benefits come from consistent practice, but you can attend as sessions allow. The take-home guide helps you practice independently.
Can I practice these techniques at home?
Absolutely! That's the whole point. You'll receive a complete home practice guide to use whenever you want—morning, evening, or whenever you need calm and focus.
How quickly will I see results?
Some people feel calmer after the first session. Others notice improved sleep and focus after a week of regular practice. Consistent practice over 4-6 weeks shows the most significant benefits.
What locations do you conduct workshops?
We conduct workshops in Satara, Mahad, and Pune. Please contact us for the next scheduled session in your area.
How do I register or get more details?
Click "Register Now" or "WhatsApp Enquiry" to get in touch. We'll share workshop dates, timings, venue details, and pricing information with you.
Is there a payment plan or group discount?
Yes! We offer flexible payment options and special discounts for groups. Connect with us for personalized pricing.
