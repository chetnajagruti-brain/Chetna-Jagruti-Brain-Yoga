Our programs are designed for children aged 6-16 years. However, we customize approaches based on individual developmental stages. Consult us for specific age-related guidance.

Many parents notice improvements in concentration and focus within 2-3 weeks. Academic improvements typically show within 1-3 months of consistent practice. Results vary based on individual commitment and practice frequency.

Yes! Our programs are based on established neuroscience research on brain development, neuroplasticity, and learning optimization. Each program has been refined through years of real-world application with thousands of children.
