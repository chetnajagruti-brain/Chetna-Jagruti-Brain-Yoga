What is Brain Yoga?

Brain Yoga is an ancient practice rooted in Indian wisdom, now validated by modern neuroscience. It combines gentle physical movements, breathing techniques (pranayama), and mindfulness practices specifically designed to stimulate different regions of the brain, enhance cognitive abilities, and promote overall mental wellness.

Unlike traditional yoga that focuses on physical flexibility, Brain Yoga is specifically targeted toward improving mental function, concentration, memory, and emotional regulation.
