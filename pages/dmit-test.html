Dermatoglyphics Multiple Intelligence Test(DMIT)

What is DMIT?

The Dermatoglyphics Multiple Intelligence Test(DMIT) is a scientifically-designed assessment tool developed by Chetna Jagruti to measure your mental well-being, cognitive abilities, and mindfulness levels. It provides a comprehensive evaluation of your brain's performance across multiple dimensions including focus, memory, emotional intelligence, and stress resilience.

Unlike traditional IQ tests, DMIT doesn't just measure raw intelligence - it evaluates practical mental fitness and your ability to apply mindfulness in daily life.
