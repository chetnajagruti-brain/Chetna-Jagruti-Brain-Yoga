Brain Yoga: The Science of Mental Fitness

What is Brain Yoga?

Brain Yoga is a comprehensive system of mental exercises, breathing techniques, and mindfulness practices designed to enhance cognitive function, improve focus, and promote emotional well-being. It combines ancient yogic principles with modern neuroscience to create a practical approach to mental fitness.

Unlike traditional yoga that focuses primarily on physical flexibility, Brain Yoga specifically targets mental clarity, emotional balance, and cognitive enhancement through evidence-based practices.
