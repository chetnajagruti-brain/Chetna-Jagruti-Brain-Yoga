❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is brain yoga suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! Brain yoga is designed for all fitness levels. Our beginner programs start with simple exercises and gradually increase in complexity.

How long before I see results?

Most people notice improvements in focus and stress levels within 2-3 weeks of consistent practice. Significant changes typically appear within 8-12 weeks.

Do I need any equipment?

No! Brain yoga requires no equipment. You just need a quiet space and 10-15 minutes to practice.

Can I practice brain yoga at home?

Yes! Many of our exercises can be done at home. However, learning from an expert instructor is recommended, especially for beginners.

Is it safe for people with health conditions?

Generally, yes. However, consult your doctor before starting, especially if you have serious health conditions or injuries.

How often should I practice?

For best results, practice 3-4 times per week. Daily practice delivers faster and more significant results.
