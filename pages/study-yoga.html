What is Study Yoga?

Study Yoga is a specialized application of Brain Yoga designed specifically for students of all levels - from primary school through university and professional courses. It combines focused mental exercises, attention enhancement techniques, and stress management practices to optimize learning capacity and academic performance.

Unlike generic study tips, Study Yoga works at the neurological level, literally rewiring your brain for better focus, faster memorization, and improved exam performance.
