Brain Mindfulness Intelligence Test (BMIT)

What is BMIT?

The Brain Mindfulness Intelligence Test (BMIT) is a scientifically-designed assessment tool developed by Chetna Jagruti to measure your mental well-being, cognitive abilities, and mindfulness levels. It provides a comprehensive evaluation of your brain's performance across multiple dimensions including focus, memory, emotional intelligence, and stress resilience.

Unlike traditional IQ tests, BMIT doesn't just measure raw intelligence - it evaluates practical mental fitness and your ability to apply mindfulness in daily life.

Why Take the DMIT Test?

  • Establish a baseline for your current mental well-being
  • Identify specific areas where brain yoga can help you most
  • Track your progress over time with quantifiable metrics
  • Receive personalized recommendations based on your results
  • Motivate yourself with concrete benchmarks
  • Understand your cognitive strengths and areas for improvement
  • Access a comprehensive wellness roadmap

DMIT Test Parameters

The comprehensive DMIT test evaluates the following key dimensions:

🧠 Cognitive Focus

Measures your ability to concentrate and maintain attention on tasks

💾 Memory Capacity

Evaluates both short-term and long-term memory retention abilities

😌 Mindfulness Level

Assesses your present-moment awareness and emotional consciousness

🛡️ Stress Resilience

Measures your ability to handle stress and maintain mental balance

❤️ Emotional Intelligence

Evaluates your understanding and management of emotions

⚡ Mental Energy

Assesses your mental vitality and cognitive stamina throughout the day
